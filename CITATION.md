# Citing tilepy

If you use tilepy in your research, please include the following sentence in your manuscript:

> This work made use of the tilepy observation scheduling platform (Seglar-Arroyo et al. 2024).

## Primary reference

**M. Seglar-Arroyo, H. Ashkar, M. de Bony de Lavergne, F. Schüssler**,
*Cross-Observatory Coordination with tilepy: A Novel Tool for Observations of Multi-Messenger Transient Events*,
The Astrophysical Journal Supplement Series, 274, 1 (2024).
DOI: [10.3847/1538-4365/ad5bde](https://doi.org/10.3847/1538-4365/ad5bde) — [arXiv:2407.18076](https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.18076)

```bibtex
@article{SeglarArroyo2024,
  author        = {Seglar-Arroyo, Monica and Ashkar, Halim and
                   {de Bony de Lavergne}, Mathieu and Sch{\"u}ssler, Fabian},
  title         = {Cross-Observatory Coordination with tilepy: A Novel Tool
                   for Observations of Multi-Messenger Transient Events},
  journal       = {The Astrophysical Journal Supplement Series},
  year          = 2024,
  month         = sep,
  volume        = {274},
  number        = {1},
  doi           = {10.3847/1538-4365/ad5bde},
  eprint        = {2407.18076},
  archivePrefix = {arXiv},
  primaryClass  = {astro-ph.IM}
}
```
